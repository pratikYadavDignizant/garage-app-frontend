# MVP Plan & Future Roadmap

This document outlines the current Minimum Viable Product (MVP) state and the roadmap for future enhancements to make this the best garage management platform.

## Current MVP Features (Phase 1)

- [x] **Secure Authentication**: Firebase-powered Phone OTP login for garage owners.
- [x] **Dashboard**: High-level overview of services, customers, and pending reminders.
- [x] **Customer Management**: Centralized database of customers and their contact details.
- [x] **Vehicle Management**: Track vehicle history, plate numbers, and models.
- [x] **Service Recording**: Log service dates and details for each vehicle.
- [x] **Automated Reminders**: Integration with WhatsApp for service notifications.
- [x] **Templates**: Customizable message templates for different service types.

## Proposed Enhancements (Phase 2)

### Inventory Management

- [ ] Track stock levels for common parts (filters, oils, spark plugs).
- [ ] Low stock alerts and automated reordering.

### Billing & Invoicing

- [ ] Generate professional PDF invoices directly from service records.
- [ ] Track payments and outstanding balances.
- [ ] Integration with payment gateways (Stripe, UPI, etc.).

### Advanced Analytics

- [ ] Revenue tracking over time.
- [ ] Customer retention metrics.
- [ ] Daily/Weekly/Monthly performance reports.

### Multi-Staff Support

- [ ] Role-based access control (RBAC) for mechanics and front-desk staff.
- [ ] Activity logs for audit trails.

## The "Best Version" Goals (Phase 3)

- **Customer Facing Portal**: A simple PWA for car owners to view their service history and book appointments.
- **Mobile App**: Native Android/iOS apps for garage owners for on-the-go management.
- **AI-Powered Predictions**: Predict peak service times and suggest proactive maintenance to customers based on vehicle age/mileage.
- **Third-Party Integrations**: Direct connection to local parts suppliers for instant ordering.
